Privacy Policy for CronPost

Last Updated: May 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CronPost ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service (the "Service").

By using CronPost, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. The security of your data is paramount to us, and we want to be transparent about how we handle it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information solely for the purpose of providing and improving the Service to you. We do not use your information for any other purposes.

2.1. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your email address and a hashed version of your password. We never store your plain text password.
  • Scheduled Messages: We store the content of the messages you create, the recipient information (e.g., email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact details you provide), and the schedule you set for these messages. This information is treated as highly confidential.
  • Check-in Configuration: We store your preferences for check-in schedules and any related settings required for the Service to monitor your activity status.

2.2. Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign up or sign in using Google, we will receive certain profile information from Google, such as your name, email address, and profile picture, as permitted by Google and your Google account settings. We only use this information for authentication and to populate your account details within CronPost.

2.3. Usage Data (Log Data)

We may collect minimal log data necessary for the operation and security of the Service, such as access times and IP addresses for security monitoring and troubleshooting. This data is not used to track your behavior for other purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to provide, maintain, and improve the CronPost service. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Create and manage your CronPost account.
  • Enable you to create, schedule, and manage your messages.
  • Monitor your check-in schedule as configured by you.
  • Deliver your scheduled messages to your designated recipients if your check-in conditions are met (i.e., inactivity is detected according to your settings).
  • Send you service-related communications, such as account verification emails, check-in reminders (if configured), and notifications regarding your scheduled messages or account status.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our Service.

We explicitly state that the content of your scheduled messages and recipient details are confidential and will not be accessed, read, or used by us for any purpose other than the automated delivery as part of the Service you have configured. We will not use your personal information or message content for any advertising, marketing, or other unrelated purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We value your privacy and limit the sharing of your information.

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services to help us operate CronPost. These include:
    • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider for database storage, authentication, and other backend functionalities. Your data, including scheduled messages, is stored with Supabase.
    • Cloudflare: Used for DNS, network security, and performance (including Cloudflare Tunnel and Turnstile).
    • Email Delivery Service (e.g., Tenten/emailserver.net.vn): Used to send service-related emails (like verifications and your scheduled messages) from `noreply@cronpost.com`.
    These service providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their functions and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not and will not sell your personal information or message content to third parties.
  • No Sharing for Third-Party Marketing: We do not share your personal information or message content with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency). We will strive to notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.

5. Security of Your Information

The security of your data is a top priority for us. We implement and maintain reasonable administrative, physical, and technical security safeguards to help protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Measures include:

  • Using secure backend services like Supabase, which provides robust security features for data at rest and in transit (e.g., SSL/TLS encryption).
  • Hashing passwords before storage.
  • Implementing CAPTCHA (Cloudflare Turnstile) to protect against bot abuse.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information and scheduled messages only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, primarily as long as your account is active or your messages are pending delivery according to your schedule.

Upon your request for account deletion, or after your messages have been sent or deleted by you, we will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize your personal information in accordance with our data retention policies and applicable law, unless we are legally required to retain it.

7. Your Data Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information. You can typically manage your account information and scheduled messages directly through your account settings within the CronPost service. For other requests, such as accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal data, or if you have any questions about your data, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or a higher age threshold if stipulated by local laws). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under this age. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
